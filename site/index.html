03 / Built to be honest

Useful even when certainty is impossible.

Unknown stays unknown.

RoleAtlas never converts unclear work authorization or location eligibility into a confident green light.

AI is optional.

Local ranking and editable plans keep working without sending a résumé to an external provider.

Coverage is measured.

Configured and successfully checked sources are reported—not vague claims of the whole market.

Your history persists.

Search revisions, feedback, saved roles, and application activity survive every return visit.
